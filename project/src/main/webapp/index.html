Tell Us About Yourself

Fill out a user profile and tell us about your personal and volunteering interests. This will tell us what events you're looking to participate or volunteer in. We'll use this information to help personalize your events discovery experience.


Find Personalized Events

Discover events through our event discovery page or search for the event you're looking for. We use a discovery algorithm to personalize your feed and show you related events that you may be interested in. The search uses NLP techniques to find related events and ranks them based on their relevance to you. Sign up to check out what’s going on in your local community!


Host Your Own Events

Want to advertise your event? Looking for volunteers with certain skills? Host your own event and advertise it with LEO -- we'll make it easy and help you find participants or volunteers with the skill set needed.
